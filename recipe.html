Creamy Tomato Tortellini

Description

Tasty Pasta with a hint of spicy!

Ingredients:

  • Large saucepan, large pot, and associated cooking utencils
  • 1 Jar (12 or more ounces) of your favorite Tomato sauce as a base
  • 1 Pint heavy cream
  • 1 Package of Tortellini
  • 1 Package of meatballs *recommended but optional*
  • Spices: Chili powder, Paprika, Garlic Powder

Directions:

  1. Start by bringing water to a boil and heating up the sauce pan on medium heat.
  2. Pour the tomato sauce and cream into the saucepan so it heats up while you wait for the water to boil.
  3. Mix the spices into the sauce during this period to give the flavors time to blend. Season to taste.
  4. If you have meatballs add them to the sauce, stirring occasionally.
  5. Once the water is boiling, cook the pasta as specified on the package.
  6. After the noodles are done cooking, add them to the sauce and enjoy!
